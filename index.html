Explore the most alien looking place on earth.

  • Safe

    Socotra is not only safe, it is one of the most isolated landforms on Earth and is recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

  • Unique

    Famous for its unique Dragon Blood Trees, white sandy beaches boasting waters in every shade of blue to magical sand dunes and much more.

  • Jewel Of Biodiversity

    Socotra is considered the jewel of biodiversity in the Arabian Sea with nearly 700 endemic species that are found nowhere else on Earth.

  • Magical Place

    It is a magical place that will allow you to switch off, connect with nature, and reconnect with yourself.

  • Notes

    - The island is 125km long and 45km from North to South. You cannot book flights online and can only visit with a third-party operator.


    - While English is not widely spoken on the island, we have a dedicated local team to guide you around the island and show you the true magic of the island! The team will also set up camp for you, cook for you, and drive you from site to site.
